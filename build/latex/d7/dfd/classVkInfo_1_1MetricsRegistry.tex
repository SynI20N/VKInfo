\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Registry for managing and storing metrics. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classVkInfo_1_1MetricsRegistry}{Metrics\+Registry}} maintains a thread-\/safe collection of metrics. It allows registration of new metrics and provides access to all registered metrics.
